TRULY DECENTRALIZED

The e2 chain uses Deeper’s unique Proof of Credit (PoC) consensus mechanism to become a truly decentralized public blockchain. Moreover, the e2 chain is characterized by high efficiency, low energy consumption, and security. It is a next generation of public blockchains. After the launch of the e2 chain mainnet, Deeper Connect users can automatically participate in e2 chain mining, provided they meet the minimum credit score requirement.

FAIR SHARE OF SHARING ECONOMY

Deeper proposes an innovative consensus algorithm called PoC (Proof of Credit) that emulates the prestigious credit system in our daily life. PoC guarantees every participant shares equal chance to earn benefits from Deeper Network as long as they keep contributing to our network and accumulating credits. It prevents one class of participants from dominating over others. The more bandwidth a device shares, the more credit score will be earned by the account.

WORLD'S FIRST LOCK-FREE NETWORK OPERATING SYSTEM

AtomOS is a network operating system custom-built for deep security. It is also the world’s first lock-free network operating system. The advanced design of AtomOS is the foundation of the reliability, efficiency, and security of the entire system.
By means of the original HIPE data structure and its lock-free design, multiple CPUs can filter and subcontract data packets making their processing highly scalable. It also strictly guarantees that data packet arrangement is consistent with its original order
It uses zero-copy technology to read data packets directly from network devices and hand them over for processing. This increases data packet forwarding speeds tenfold, as compared to traditional operating systems (such as Linux, Freedsd, etc.)
It can perform the function of a 7-layer deep high-speed filtering of data packets on general hardware reducing the cost by 10 times as compared to professional hardware used by traditional network firewalls.

SECURE COMMUNICATION PROTOCOL

The Trident protocol is a decentralized shared communication protocol based on blockchain technology. It can circumvent network censorship transmitting data securely and maximizing the use of network bandwidth and reducing delays in the data packet transmission process. This is achieved thanks to the efficient integration of network technologies such as intranet penetration, data encryption, protocol camouflage, and tunnel layer congestion control.

FULL-THROUGHPUT

AtomOS integrates an application identification engine that is reliable, efficient, and scalable. It identifies common network traffic and performs flow control or smart routing to optimize the user experience for key applications. Also, it guarantees smooth tunnel service without consuming excessive local resources.

FAQ

How can I prevent other users from using my IP address to do illegal activities? 

(i) Users have the option to share it's bandwidth or not. (ii) DCM can recognize most internet protocols, users can choose to deny the dangerous ones. Remember DCM is also a l7 firewall. (iii) DCM can distinguish user's own connections and sharing connections. If you didn't do something bad, DCM can prove it.

What is protecting me from other people on the network attacking my device/server? 

We will give users the option not to accept dangerous connections such as BT. DCM uses a transparent mode, it only accepts connections from other DCMs, so there should not be any DDOS concerns.

Can I adjust the amount of download and upload that I want to share?

We plan to add a QoS function to limit the sharing speed and increase the priority of your Internet speed.

When sharing my bandwidth, will my internet speed get slower?

No, your Internet speed will not get slower because you are just sharing your upside bandwidth.

If I am the only user on the network what kind of speed would I be looking at instead of the theoretical maximum?

We already have thousands of nodes in the world. At the very beginning, we set up some cloud nodes around the world.

Can I do torrenting with a different IP address, but also refusing others to use my IP address for torrenting?

Deeper Network will also provide some cloud DPN nodes which will allow all kinds of traffic in the future.

Could you please clarify your answer to the question of “ No Logging of your DPN” ?

That information is not stored in any server and we do not have access to it. You are the only person that has access to that information and in order to access it you need to connect to your device through AtomOS using your personal password. Even if the device is stolen physically, nobody will be able to access it, unless they know your password.

How do you achieve your high speed connection?

Through our AtomOS lock-free networking operational system, advanced congestion control protocol, and Smart VPN routing powered by Deeper network.

How stable is your system? Will there be network interruptions as seen in other VPNs?

Compared to traditional VPNs that are supported by a centralized network (where taking down the central servers will paralyze the network), DPN is a decentralized network that will not have a Single Point Failure during the service. DPN can also survive IP address blocking, port blocking, and pattern detection.

Why would I need an enterprise level security device? For most PCs, and laptops, isn’t it enough to have an antivirus protection software or the security system provided by Windows or MacOS?

Enterprise level security will guarantee a secure network that protects all devices in your households or small businesses, particularly IoT devices that are normally overlooked.

But VPN services usually have hundreds of servers located across the globe which makes them effectively decentralized

They can have thousands of servers, but at the end of the day all the servers are controlled by the VPN provider, that is what centralization means. They can sniffer or steal any data they want. That is something impossible to do in a decentralized private network since there are no servers and no central authority in charge.

How is this device decentralized or what is the mechanism that is currently using to achieve decentralization?

Every device can share its bandwidth with other devices using DHT and blockchain to discover each other. This is something users can verify by themselves using our AtomOS interface.

What is the mechanism that protects users from becoming an exit node?

Users can disable some P2P protocol to prevent other nodes using its bandwidth to download unauthorized content. We use source portals to distinguish different types of traffic.

How many active nodes do you have and where are they located?

We have more than 1000 nodes all over the world. They are in Asia, North America and Europe.

Which OS does your device run?

It runs AtomOS, the world’s first lock-free operating system, designed exclusively to run on Deeper Connect. Those with a curious heart will find deep technical details in our whitepaper.
